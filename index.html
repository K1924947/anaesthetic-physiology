About
Built as a personal project while preparing for anaesthetic training — the tools reflect topics I wanted to understand at a mechanistic level rather than just memorise. Each visualisation is designed to make the underlying physiology navigable rather than just presentable.
For whom
Written with medical students preparing for Step 1 and finals, foundation doctors consolidating preclinical knowledge, and anaesthetic trainees preparing for the FRCA in mind. All tools run entirely in the browser — no account, no download.
